# Refund Policy

Customers may request a full refund within 30 days of purchase, provided the
item is in original condition. Refunds are processed to the original payment
method within 5-7 business days of receipt of the returned item.

For digital goods, refunds are available within 14 days of purchase, provided
the customer has not downloaded the content more than three times.

Subscription services may be cancelled at any time. Refunds for the unused
portion of a subscription period are pro-rated and issued within 10 business
days.

To initiate a refund, customers should contact support@example.com with their
order number. Refund requests are typically acknowledged within 1 business day.
